Cosmos



You are a blue ball in a galaxy! Use your arrow keys or WASD to move.

Avoid white bubbles flyng in. They can come from anywhere!

TWO WAYS TO PLAY

RUNAWAY - Focus on just evading attackers by running away

SNIPER - avaoid attackers and shoot at the invaders by clicking!

M = Mute Music

R = Restart Game

P = Pause Game

1 = Runaway

2 = Sniper


Press ENTER now to start!
